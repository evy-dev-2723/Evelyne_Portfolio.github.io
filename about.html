About Me

Get to know my journey, passion, and vision

Hello, I'm Evelyne

I am a passionate and ambitious Software Developer with a unique journey that blends technology, creativity, and leadership. Before diving into the world of programming, I proudly represented my country by winning the prestigious title of Miss Cultures Africaines Top Model in Brazzaville. My background in modeling and cultural ambassadorship taught me confidence, discipline, public speaking, and the power of representing something greater than myself.

Along my journey, I also pursued my love for technology. I studied Cybersecurity and earned recognition and awards that encouraged me to go further. Today, I am continuing my education in Software Development, focusing on building meaningful digital solutions while shaping a strong technical future.

My Background

I come from a diverse path: crowned as a cultural ambassador, trained in cybersecurity, and now evolving as a software developer. This combination allows me to merge discipline, creativity, and technical expertise into everything I build.

My Approach

I believe in creating clean, efficient, and beautiful digital experiences. Whether it's through web design, coding logic, or user-centered interfaces, I aim to connect innovation with meaningful purpose.

My Mission

My mission is to inspire, innovate, and empower — especially young African women in tech. I want to use my skills and my platform to show that you can shine in culture, beauty, intelligence, and technology all at once.
